ACP Privacy Policy

We are committed to providing the best services to all visitors to our website (“you”, “your”). This includes respecting your privacy and ensuring that we take all reasonable measures to safeguard your information and data including personal data (the "data") in accordance with the applicable regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. This Privacy Policy has been made available to you for you to understand how we may process, collect, use, treat, and protect your data when you visit our Site, and other platforms that may require processing of data in whatever form (the "Platforms").

What does this policy contain?

This privacy policy explains how the Program will use your data through interactions on the website. It also outlines your rights in protecting your data, including your right to object to some of the methods we use in data processing.

What kind of data do we collect

The Program collects your data when you interact with its website, directly with the Program, and/or when you share information related to your profile, such as username, full name, email address, phone number, notification preferences, language, or through any other documents uploaded.

How do we collect your personal information?

Most of the data we collect and process are data that you provide directly to the Program. This occurs when you interact with the Program's website, register your profile, or submit any form or request on the Program's website.

Why do we collect data?

The purposes for which we collect data include the following:

  • Obtaining your feedback on our services
  • Delivering customized digital experiences
  • Enhancing user profiles
  • Identifying users
  • Targeting the right audience with promotional campaigns
  • Developing and organizing plans related to ACP's activities
  • Monitoring website usage patterns
  • Sharing and receiving partnership opportunities
  • Providing potential services to you
  • Enabling you to evaluate our platform and services

The Program may share your data with external parties such as partners, government entities, and service providers.

How do we process your data?

The Program uses specific tools to process, analyze, and make decisions regarding your data.

What is the legal basis for processing personal data?

This Privacy Policy has been created under the regulations put forth by the Saudi Data and AI Authority's (SDAIA) Personal Data Protection Law, as well as but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the National Data Management Office (NDMO) Personal Data Protection Policy.

How do we store your data?

The Program stores personal data, whether obtained manually or automatically, in accordance with the best practices adopted in the tourism and aviation sectors. Your data will be retained only as long as it is necessary. It will be deleted once it no longer serves a purpose. Upon request, your data will be promptly deleted, unless legal obligations require us to continue storing it.

What are your rights in protecting your data?

The Program respects your rights in protecting your data in accordance with the personal data protection regulations, provided that this does not conflict with applicable laws and regulations. Users have the following rights:

  • Right to awareness: Users have the right to be informed of the legal basis for collecting their data, the purpose of such collection, and the related regulations, procedures, and policies.
  • Right to access: Users have the right to access their data held by the Program.
  • Right to obtain: Users have the right to access their data held by the Program in a readable format.
  • Right to correction: Users have the right to request correcting or updating personal data by the Program.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Users have the right to withdraw their consent for the processing of their data at any time.
  • Right to restrict processing: Users have the right to request the restriction of processing of their data under certain conditions.
  • Right to data portability: Users have the right to request the transfer of their data to another entity or directly to them under certain conditions.
  • Right to object: Users have the right to object to the processing of their data held by the Program.
  • Right to erasure: Users have the right to request the erasure of their data held by the Program when it is no longer needed, provided that this does not affect the Program's operations or the purpose of data collection.

The Program has a 60-business day deadline to respond to any requests related to the above rights. Requests should be submitted through the Program's communication channels.

Amendments to the Privacy Policy

The Program reserves the right to modify and revise the privacy policy without prior notice. Last updated privacy policy on [18 December 2023].

Contact us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, or your data, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights related to the protection of your data, please contact us via email at: communication@acp.gov.sa.

Cookie Usage Policy

This policy explains how the Program's website uses cookies. By using this website, you explicitly agree to the placement of cookies on your computer device in accordance with the terms of this policy, as we use these cookies in the Program to enhance the website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that contain small pieces of data, such as a username and password, which are stored client-side when a user browses the website.

How Does the Program Use Cookies?

Information you provide: You can provide us with information about yourself by filling out the forms on the Program's website, or by contacting us via phone, email, or other means of communication. This includes information you provide when registering on the website, creating your user profile, sending us an email, or reporting an issue with the website.

Information automatically collected by the Program during your website visit: Technical information, including your Internet Protocol (IP) address, which is used to connect your device to the internet, your login information, the type and version of your browser, your time zone settings, the browser you use, any add-ons, and the operating platform of your internet system.

Information about your website visit: The URL, which is the internet site address, tracking your click-through path on and through the site (including date and time), information related to page response times, download errors, the duration of your visits to specific pages, your interaction with the content displayed on the page (such as clicking on or moving it on the page), and the methods used for browsing outside the pages.

Information received by the Program from other sources: The Program may receive information about you if you use any of the other sites operated by the Program or any of the other services it offers.

Cookies used by the airline link Program: The website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of the site, which helps us provide you with a unique experience during your visit to our site. Cookies are stored by the Program in your browser or on your device's hard drive when you agree to it.

We use the following types of Cookies:

  • Performance/analytical cookies: These cookies allow us to recognize visitors, count the number of visitors, and analyze usage patterns on the website. This helps us improve the website's functionality and ensures users find what they are looking for easily and conveniently.
  • Functional cookies: These cookies are used to recognize you when you return to the website, enabling us to tailor the site's content to your preferences and create a personalized user experience.
  • Targeting cookies: These cookies record your visits to the website, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We use this information to make the website more relevant to your interests. Additionally, we may share this information with third parties for the same purpose.

Please note that third parties may also use cookies that are not controlled by the Program. These cookies are likely to be performance, analytical, or targeting cookies. You can block cookies by enabling the option to reject all or some of these cookies in your browser settings. However, by blocking the use of all cookies (including essential cookies), you may not be able to access all or some parts of the website.
